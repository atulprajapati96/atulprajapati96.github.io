About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Astroparticle Physics working on advanced gaseous detectors for directional dark matter searches. My research combines detector development, signal processing, computer vision, and machine learning to reconstruct particle tracks in three dimensions using camera and PMT data. I actively contribute to detector R&D activities at LNGS, including studies involving electroluminescence, GEM-based amplification systems, negative ion drift gases, and next-generation dark matter experiments.

Research Interests
